Public Sector Projects

Collaborating with Schools, Universities, State and Local Governments in creating high performing facilities, better community and environmentally sound spaces

Main Hall

Location: Virginia School for the Deaf and the Blind, Staunton, VA
Original Date of Construction: 1845
Building Use: VSDB Administatioin Building
Highlights: Listed on National Register of Historic Places and Virginia Landmarks Register in 1969
Collaboration: Collaboration with Preservation Design Partnership (PDP) of Philadelphia, the Commonwealth of Virginia, and the Virginia School for the Deaf and the Blind (VSDB). Not executive architect.

Bradford Hall

Location: Virginia School for the Deaf and the Blind, Staunton, VA

Highlights: Renovation of historic building on VSDB campus

Garrett Hall
